# Research and Problem Analysis

## Product Context

Patent Express is a service that helps innovators file patents through a simplified process with expert guidance.

The goal of the platform is to help individuals and startups protect their intellectual property quickly and efficiently.

---

## Target Users

- Startup founders
- Developers and engineers
- Researchers
- Product innovators
- Students working on inventions

These users may have valuable ideas but limited knowledge of the patent filing process.

---

## User Goals

Users want to:

- Protect their invention legally
- Prevent competitors from copying their ideas
- Increase credibility with investors
- Secure intellectual property rights

---

## Key Problems Identified

### 1. Unclear Service Explanation

First-time visitors may not immediately understand what Patent Express offers or how the process works.

---

### 2. Complex Lead Generation

Long forms can create friction and discourage users from starting the patent application process.

---

### 3. Weak Conversion Path

The transition from discovery to application is not always clearly guided.

---

### 4. Lack of Trust Signals

Legal services require strong credibility indicators such as testimonials, guarantees, and transparent pricing.

---

## Design Goals

The redesign focuses on:

- Improving service clarity
- Simplifying lead capture
- Reducing cognitive load
- Building trust
- Creating a clear conversion path

---

## UX Strategy

The redesigned journey follows a structured flow:

Landing Page → Lead Generation → Checkout

This structure guides users step-by-step through the process while minimizing confusion.
